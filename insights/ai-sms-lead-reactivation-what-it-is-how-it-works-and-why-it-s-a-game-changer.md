## First Off, What *Is* AI SMS Lead Reactivation?

It’s pretty simple.

We use a AI-Powered agent that reaches out to your **old, cold, or unconverted leads** via SMS, starts a conversation, qualifies their intent, and either books a meeting, converts a sale or alerts you when they’re interested.

Think of it as **a smart follow-up machine** that sounds like a real person—and doesn’t need sleep, coffee, or PTO.

## Do I Need to Be Techy to Use This?

Not at all.

Here’s how easy it is to get started:

- 
**Upload your leads** into your private account (CSV works great).

- 
Or, if you’re using a CRM, we can **sync it via API**—just let us know what you’re using.

- 
Your AI assistant starts reaching out **within a few days**, qualifying leads and sending you real-time alerts when someone bites.

You don’t need to write code, understand automation, or even log in every day. It’s built to run **on autopilot**, while you focus on closing deals.

## Can I Customize the AI?

Absolutely. We believe your AI should sound like *you*.

- 
**Assistant identity**: Choose if the AI messages leads as you, or as an assistant.

- 
**Opening message**: Use our high-performing template or write your own.

- 
**Outreach goal**: Want it to book meetings? Or just find out who’s in the market again? You decide.

- 
**Phone number**: It texts from a number we register with you and keeps conversations accessible through your dashboard.

## Will I Be Notified When a Lead Replies?

Yes. You can be notified anytime someone replies, gets qualified, or asks a question the AI can’t answer.

You’ll never miss a hot lead again—even if you’re away from your desk.

And yes, you can **view every conversation** inside your dashboard.

You’re always in the loop.

## Are My Leads Private?

Absolutely.

We never share, sell, or use your leads with anyone else. They’re 100% your property—and that’s how it should be.

## What Happens if the AI Gets Stumped?

Great question. 

If a lead asks something the AI doesn’t know how to answer, you’ll get a heads-up.
We’ll notify you so you can jump in and pick up the conversation manually.

It’s a team effort—you and your AI assistant working in sync.
