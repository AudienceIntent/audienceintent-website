## **Exactly What to Do With Your Old Leads (Step-by-Step)**

Let’s get tactical.
Here’s the play that works — every single time.

## **1. Clean your data**

Duplicates, missing phone numbers, bad emails, junk tags.
If your CRM looks like a yard sale, nothing works.

Clean it once → profit from it forever.

## **2. Segment by Intent**

Your CRM isn’t one big blob of contacts.
You’ve got:

- people who almost bought

- people who asked for info

- people who went cold mid-convo

- past customers

- long-lost “maybe later” people

- abandoned consults/applications

Each bucket needs a different approach.

## **3. Restart conversations through SMS — not email**

Email is a graveyard.
SMS is where humans live.

That’s why SMS gets:

- 90%+ open rates

- 35–50% response rates

- same-day bookings

When you start the convo the right way, old leads wake up FAST.

## **4. Use AI to scale natural conversation**

Not bots.
Not scripts.
Not a “blast.”

You need actual **back-and-forth conversation** that feels human — instantly, at scale.

This is the part businesses suck at doing internally.

Your team doesn’t have the time.
Automation tools feel robotic.
Cold blasts feel aggressive.

AI-texting that feels like a real person?
Game changer.

## **5. Make an offer that fits their original intent**

Not discounts.
Not desperate pitches.
Not “Are you still interested?”

Example reactivation angles:

- “Want help finishing what you started?”

- “Looks like you were exploring ____ — want to revisit?”

- “If timing is better now, I can help.”

- “Need an updated recommendation?”

- “Ready to pick this back up?”

This removes pressure and puts them in the driver’s seat.

## **6. Make it stupid-easy to book**

One-click calendar link.
One clear next step.
No friction.

Humans convert when things are simple.
Old leads convert when things are effortless.
